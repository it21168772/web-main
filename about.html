15 Years of Experience

Welcome to Chaminda Tours, where we combine 15 years of tour expertise with exceptional driving skills and registration with the Sri Lanka Tourism Board. Our English-speaking driver is not just a guide; they're your friendly travel companion, making your journey informative and enjoyable. They are dedicated to providing a personalized experience, answering your questions, offering recommendations, and ensuring your comfort throughout the trip. What truly sets us apart is our commitment to hospitality. From the moment you step into our comfortable vehicle, you're treated like family. We believe that hospitality is a way of life, and we ensure that you experience the warm and welcoming culture of Sri Lanka firsthand.

Your safety and comfort are our top priorities. Our well-maintained vehicles guarantee a smooth and secure ride, allowing you to fully enjoy the breathtaking sights of Sri Lanka. With Chaminda Tours, you're not just booking a tour; you're embarking on a journey filled with lasting memories. We invite you to explore the enchanting beauty of Sri Lanka while experiencing the warmth of our hospitality, the expertise of our driver, and the assurance of a comfortable and safe ride. Join us on this extraordinary adventure!
